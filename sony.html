The Xperience

One of the best parts about the internet and websites is interactivity. A website that engages the user and encourages them to participate in the experience of gathering information, is far more likely to make an impression.

The task was to create a website that doesn’t just relay information and show images – it is an experience to delve into. With the Sony Xperia X1 I managed to create a web that showcases some of the best features that the phone has to offer.
